Automotive Connected Experience
In-Vehicle and Mobile App Design for Mass Market Vehicle

Project Overview

This was a project for General Motors as part of the Auto UX class (SI594). The goal was to design user interfaces focusing on cost-conscious users' comfort, convenience, and safety. One of our key achievements was the implementation of a unique Pet Mode for pet owners, ensuring a safe environment for pets left in the vehicle. Our group's design was recognized as the "most improved" by the professor, highlighting the significant progress and innovation we brought to the project.

Problem

The mass market segment includes a broad range of users with varied needs. The solution needed to balance affordability with a rich feature set, ensuring user satisfaction across different personas.

Solution

We designed an intuitive HMI and mobile app, featuring key innovations like Pet Mode for pet safety. The design focused on simplicity and easy-of-use while bringing rich features, enhancing user comfort, convenience, and safety.

My Design Process

Personas

We wanted to form a deeper understanding of our users' goals, needs, experiences, and behaviors. So, we created 3 personas for each of our user segments. We used these personas whenever we wanted to step out of ourselves and reconsider our initial ideas.

  • Young Professionals: Value convenience and connectivity.

  • Pet Owners: Require solutions for traveling with pets.

  • Tech Novice: Need a simple and intuitive interface, clear instructions, minimal learning curve.

UI Design

Once the usability issues were resolved, I moved on to design the final screens in Figma. My goal was to create a visual identity thatâ€™s minimalistic, focusing on ease of use while bringing rich features.

We chose this visual identity because many mass-market sedan drivers may be unfamiliar with touch-screen infotainment systems from their previous vehicles. Our goal is to introduce these new users to a minimalistic and intuitive interface that is easy to navigate, even for those without prior experience, allowing them to find desired functions effortlessly.
